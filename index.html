ABOUT THE INITIATIVE

OUR MISSION

We are a specialized collective operating at the intersection of Neural Engineering and Kinetic Design. Project CORE is dedicated to decoding human sentiment purely through body language, moving beyond traditional, privacy-invasive facial recognition systems.

Our research focuses on identifying macro and micro-gestures that correlate with internal emotional states, creating a seamless, non-verbal communication bridge between humans and machines in next-generation digital environments.

SYSTEM SPECIFICATIONS

POSE_NET ARCHITECTURE

Powered by a customized lightweight PoseNet model running locally via TensorFlow.js. Maps 17 distinct skeletal vertices at up to 60 FPS directly within the browser environment.

KINETIC PARADIGMS

Currently training to identify three foundational emotional paradigms based on spatial coordination: CALM (Baseline Homeostasis), ANXIOUS (Kinetic Overload), and HAPPY (Spatial Expansion).

EDGE COMPUTING

100% Client-Side Processing. No biometric or video data is ever transmitted to external servers, ensuring absolute operator privacy during the neural link session.

CALIBRATION PROTOCOL

Operators must maintain a distance of 2-3 meters from the sensor. Hold each pose for 2 seconds for neural matrix synchronization.

[01] CALM (BASELINE)

Neutral, relaxed posture. Arms hang naturally at sides. Static or minimal movement. Mapped as biological homeostasis.

[02] ANXIOUS (STRESS)

Closed posture. Restless inward motions. Hands touching face or head. Leaning forward. Mapped as kinetic overload.

[03] HAPPY (EUPHORIA)

Expansive, energetic motions. Arms raised high or dancing. Head tilted upward. Mapped as spatial expansion.
