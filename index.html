ABOUT US

We are creators from a background in visual and digital media, focusing on the relationship between technology and human perception. We attempt to observe and understand emotions not through language, but through bodily movement.

By training and classifying three fundamental emotional states—Calm, Anxiety, and Happiness—we developed a system where users interact without words, relying solely on physical expression.

PROJECT DETAILS

This project approaches emotion recognition from a different perspective—using body movement as the primary basis for recognition. Developed using Google's Teachable Machine platform, the system captures real-time motion data to map kinetic patterns to emotional states.

CALIBRATION PROTOCOL

Match the kinetic references below to initiate state synchronization.

[01] CALM

Neutral, relaxed posture. Arms hang naturally at sides.

[02] ANXIOUS

Closed posture. Restless inward motions. Hands touching face.

[03] HAPPY

Expansive, energetic motions. Arms raised high or dancing.
