ABOUT US

THE DEVELOPMENT TEAM

We are a dedicated collective of neural engineers and kinetic designers exploring the boundaries of human-machine interaction. By stepping away from traditional facial recognition, our mission is to understand human emotion purely through the universal language of body movement.

Our core philosophy revolves around creating lightweight, accessible, and privacy-conscious biometric interfaces for the next generation of digital environments.

PROJECT SPECIFICATIONS

TECHNOLOGY STACK

Powered by a specialized PoseNet architecture via Teachable Machine, mapping 17 distinct skeletal vertices in real-time within the browser.

KINETIC ANALYSIS

The system distinguishes between three primary emotional paradigms: CALM (Baseline), ANXIOUS (Overload), and HAPPY (Expansion) based on spatial coordination.

PRIVACY FIRST

All video processing occurs locally on the client's GPU. No image data is transmitted to external servers during the neural link session.

CALIBRATION GUIDE

[01] CALM (BASELINE)

Maintain a neutral posture. Arms relaxed at sides. System maps this as standard biological homeostasis.

[02] ANXIOUS (STRESS)

Execute closed or repetitive inward motions. Touching face or head. Triggers system instability protocols.

[03] HAPPY (EUPHORIA)

Execute expansive, open, or energetic upward motions. Triggers dopamine detection feedback.
