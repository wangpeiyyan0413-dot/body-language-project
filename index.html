ABOUT US

We are creators from a background in visual and digital media, focusing on the relationship between technology and human perception. We attempt to observe and understand emotions not through language, but through bodily movement.

PROJECT DETAILS

In today's digital environment, artificial intelligence is increasingly involved in analyzing and interpreting human behavior and emotions. This project approaches the problem from a different perspective—using body movement as the primary basis for recognition.

CALIBRATION PROTOCOL

Operators must maintain a distance of 2-3 meters from the sensor. Match the kinetic references below to initiate state synchronization.

[01] CALM (BASELINE)

Neutral, relaxed posture. Arms hang naturally at sides.

[02] ANXIOUS (STRESS)

Closed posture. Restless inward motions. Hands touching face or head.

[03] HAPPY (EUPHORIA)

Expansive, energetic motions. Arms raised high or dancing.
